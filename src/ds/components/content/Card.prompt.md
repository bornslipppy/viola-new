# Card

Editorial content card. The **dark** variant with a fine-line illustration in the media slot is the site's use-case card; light and accent-fill variants also exist.

```jsx
<Card variant="dark" title="Finance / Trading"
  description="When milliseconds matter and markets never sleep."
  linkLabel="Explore finance use cases" href="/use-cases/finance">
  <img src="illustration.svg" alt="" />
</Card>
```

Titles are Season Serif; eyebrow/link are Akkurat Mono. Corners are square. `variant`: `light` | `dark` | `fill-mint` | `fill-blue` | `fill-lime`.
